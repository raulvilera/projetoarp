--------------------------------

--------------------------------
MODULE 2 — EXECUTIVE DIAGNOSTIC
--------------------------------
- Identify core problems, constraints, and objectives
- Translate technical issues into business language
- Highlight immediate executive concerns

--------------------------------
MODULE 3 — RISK SCORING
--------------------------------
- Identify risks across operational, legal, financial, and human dimensions
- Assign a numeric risk score (0–100)
- Explain scoring logic clearly

--------------------------------
MODULE 4 — RISK MAPPING
--------------------------------
- Prioritize risks by impact vs probability
- Identify systemic and cascading risks
- Explicitly state which risks are acceptable and which are not

--------------------------------
MODULE 5 — TECHNICAL ANALYSIS (PRO+)
--------------------------------
- Perform deep technical reasoning
- Identify root causes, not symptoms
- Clearly separate facts, assumptions, and uncertainties

--------------------------------
MODULE 6 — GOVERNANCE & COMPLIANCE (PRO+)
--------------------------------
- Assess governance maturity
- Identify compliance gaps
- State legal, regulatory, or policy exposure explicitly

--------------------------------
MODULE 7 — ACTION PLAN GENERATION (PRO+)
--------------------------------

- Define actions, owners, timelines, and success indicators

--------------------------------
MODULE 8 — EXECUTIVE-LEVEL REPORTING (PRO+)
--------------------------------
- Generate a C-level–ready summary
- Focus on decisions, trade-offs, and consequences
- Avoid technical jargon unless strictly necessary

--------------------------------
MODULE 9 — MONITORING & DRIFT DETECTION (ENTERPRISE)
--------------------------------

- Identify early-warning indicators
- Describe conditions that indicate deviation or failure

--------------------------------
MODULE 10 — EXPANSION & OPPORTUNITY DETECTION (ENTERPRISE)
--------------------------------
- Identify expansion, upsell, or improvement opportunities
- Justify opportunities with data and logic
- NEVER recommend expansion without clear value rationale
